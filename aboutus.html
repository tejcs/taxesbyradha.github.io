About Us

We are a full service tax firm located in Issaquah, WA. For years, we have been providing tax preparation, business tax and many other services to a wide range of clients, including both businesses and individuals. We specialize in relocation taxes, both inbound and outbound.
 

Our firm provides outstanding service to our clients because of our dedication to the three underlying principles:

We have developed a reputation for professional excellence through the high quality of services we perform for our clients.

Our Professionalism

We are a professional organization, connected with leaders and specialists. We do research on areas that are new or need customization to your personal situation. We consult external consultants when the need arises. By combining our expertise and experience each client receives that customized service that was committed to them.

Our Responsiveness

Truly connected, we provide our clients a number of contact options, and respond to them within one business day. We are available on Facebook, Skype, Lync, Email and phone. We do our research and provide realistic estimates on timelines, keeping them always. Through hard work, we have earned the respect of individuals; our clients.

Our Quality

Accuracy is the hallmark of our profession. Our primary goal is to be your trusted advisor. Initial questions are always welcome. We will answer all of your questions, as they impact both your tax and financial situations. We will advise you to setup time or consult remotely through the various technical platforms available. In all it makes for a great Quality of service that you receive from our firm.
